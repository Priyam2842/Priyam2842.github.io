01 · PROFILE

About

I work at the intersection of mechanical engineering and robotics software — currently completing an M.Eng. in Mechatronics and Robotics after a B.E. in Mechanical Engineering. My focus is on the perception, localization, and navigation stack: turning sensor data (LiDAR, IMU, GPS, wheel encoders) into a reliable estimate of where a robot is, and a safe, efficient plan for where it should go next.

Recent work spans an indoor elderly-assistive robot, a GPS-guided delivery robot, and an outdoor agricultural AMR competing in the Field Robot Event 2026 — giving me hands-on range across ROS/ROS2, Gazebo simulation, sensor fusion (EKF/Kalman filtering), and classical path planning (Dijkstra, A*, RANSAC-based row detection).
